Research


To lead the software engineering research in India by addressing India specific issues in an industry driven
environment.The SERC is a research and development center at IIIT Hyderabad conducting research in various
domains of software engineering. The main objectives of SERC are to promote quality software development
at universities through University Software Development Center and to operate closely with the industry by
estabilishing touch points.
